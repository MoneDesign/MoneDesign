01 — Brand

A Brand Rooted in Clarity & Confidence

VMG specialises in hyperbaric chambers — medical devices used in oxygen therapy and rehabilitation. The company's philosophy, "Moving Forward", needed to be translated into a brand identity that communicated trust, innovation, and a deep connection to health and technology. Before this project, VMG operated without a consistent visual identity.

The brand palette — Indigo and Bondi Blue — was chosen to evoke calmness, professionalism, and the clinical precision associated with medical environments, while remaining approachable and human.

02 — Website

A Digital Gateway: VMG's Landing Page

With the brand identity defined, VMG needed a digital presence to reach healthcare professionals, clinics, and distributors. The landing page was designed as a focused, credibility-building experience — communicating the company's mission, product range, and values without overwhelming visitors.

  • Clear communication of VMG's mission and values above the fold
  • Essential product information structured for medical buyers
  • Full brand consistency across every page element
