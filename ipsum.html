01 — Brand

Crafting a Distinctive & Scalable Brand

A newly founded digital marketing agency, Ipsum, approached me with a clear vision — to establish a strong brand presence that would position them as a premium player in the market from day one. The agency needed an identity that felt confident, cutting-edge, and entirely ownable in a crowded digital landscape.

The design direction drew from the digital landscape itself: grid structures, UI-inspired geometric accents, and a typography-first approach that turned the brand name into a visual asset. Every element was designed to scale — from business cards to large-format campaigns.

02 — Results & Impact

Positioned to Win

The resulting brand positioned IPSUM as a cutting-edge, premium agency — not a startup trying to look established, but a confident new voice with a clear point of view. The identity provided a solid foundation for all future marketing, digital, and client-facing materials.
