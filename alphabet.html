01 — Challenge

The Challenge

Alphabet (a BMW Group company) needed to migrate its Spanish renting site to Drupal 9 while building a completely custom renting solution tailored to the Spanish market. The challenge: preserve the international brand's visual consistency while creating a highly localised, legally compliant experience.

The platform required SSO user authentication, advanced traceable filtering, automated lead communications, and a CRM adaptation — all within Drupal 9's architecture.

02 — Requirements

Key Project Requirements

  • Maintain international brand visual consistency
  • SSO user area with personalised dashboard
  • Advanced vehicle filtering with full search trazability
  • Automated lead communications and follow-up flows
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • CRM adaptation for Spanish market requirements
  • Full Drupal 9 compatibility

03 — Process

My UX Process

1

Stakeholder Interviews

Interviews with Alphabet Spain's marketing, sales, and IT teams to align on requirements and constraints.

2

End-to-End Journey Mapping

Mapped the full user journey from vehicle discovery through enquiry, SSO login, and CRM handoff.

3

Mobile-First Design Strategy

Designed all flows mobile-first, ensuring the renting experience worked flawlessly on small screens.

4

Automated Validation & Forms

Designed lead capture forms with inline validation, conditional fields, and clear error states.

5

Automation & Communication Flow

Mapped automated email sequences and CRM triggers for each stage of the customer journey.

6

CRM Evolution & Handoff

Redesigned CRM views for the Spanish sales team and delivered full Drupal-compatible specs.

04 — Results

Results and Deliverables

  • Custom renting platform live on Drupal 9
  • SSO user area with personalised vehicle history
  • Advanced filtering with full trazability for analytics
  • Automated communication flows reducing manual follow-up
  • Mobile-first experience serving the majority of traffic
  • CRM adapted and adopted by the Spanish sales team
  • International branding preserved across all touchpoints
