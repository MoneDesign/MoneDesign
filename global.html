01 — Brand

Revitalising the Brand

Global Cover Solutions, a logistics and transportation company, came to us with a clear need: their existing brand no longer reflected the scale and ambition of their operation. The identity felt dated, inconsistent across touchpoints, and failed to communicate the trust and reliability that clients expected from a logistics partner.

Key Branding Enhancements

  • Refreshed logo with stronger typographic presence and icon clarity
  • Vibrant, professional color palette conveying trust and energy
  • Cohesive visual language across all brand touchpoints

02 — Website

Transforming the Website

With the new brand identity in place, the website needed a complete redesign. The previous site was static, hard to navigate, and didn't communicate the company's services or reach effectively. The new site needed to be dynamic, interactive, and drive enquiries.

Website Redesign Highlights

  • Dynamic homepage communicating services and reach immediately
  • Interactive elements improving engagement and time on site
  • Enhanced navigation making services and contact immediately accessible
